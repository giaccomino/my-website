the studio


OMIO is an international architectural practice that provides high quality and innovative design, leading solutions to challenging architecture, urban planning and design projects; while engaging in blending enthusiasm and maturity, experience and learning… East and West.

 

OMIO in Spain

Our international team – including architects, engineers, designers, planners, scientists and management and construction services professionals – have helped shape the world around us, building a strong reputation based on our track-record of providing technical excellence to our clients.

OMIOS’s Spain’s head office in Madrid has been established as an OMIO Global Design Centre, providing multi-disciplinary services from project conception to completion, enabling our public and private sector clients to maintain their competitiveness in global markets. As a design centre, we are uniquely equipped with cutting-edge technology, such as Building Information Modelling (BIM) and WorksDifferent Architectural Lab, allowing us to guarantee the innovation and new architectural solutions and quality for the projects we develop.

For more information, please contact: madrid@omiostudio.com

 

 

OMIO in Shanghai

As an innovative architecture and integrated design practices, OMIO Shanghai is pioneers a sustainable approach to architecture through a strikingly wide range of work.

Our commitment to research and development has allowed us to bring our combined expertise to bear on an unprecedented range of our projects around the world. Although design work tends to be focused in Madrid, our Shanghai office reflects this rich mix of cultural connections with a young cosmopolitan team, that's why in 2013 we opened our JingAn Office to provide the very best service to our Chinese clients.

For more information, please contact: shanghai@omiostudio.com
