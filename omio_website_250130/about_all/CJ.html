carlos jiménez

partner architect madrid

Graduated architect by the ETSA MADRID in 2006 with postgraduate studies in Project Management and in University College of London - The Bartlett Architecture Research and Urban Design Studies.

An architect with a broad international experience not only in Europe, but also in Australia, USA and Asia.

Specialist in the area of coordinating and managing multidisciplinary teams and to develop new architecture worldwide.

Before joining OMIO he worked for several Architecture Offices and with collaborations such as Foster and Partners, Richard Rodgers, AECOM-EDAW, and ACCIONA.
