1. Introduction

PURE Resorts is a REIT corporation, that using a pool of money raised from investors will purchase land to develop it and manage the properties.

2. Stage 1: Buying Land

At this stage we are looking for plots of land with the following requirements:

Size: Approx. 350 to 800 sqm Land acquiring type: leasehold 25+ years with renewal options. High-end areas: Canggu, Cemagi, Berawa, Bingin, Seseh, Wuluwatu, etc. Walking distance to (at least one of them):

  • the beach, seaside or a major landmark.
  • amenities such as sports clubs, beach clubs, etc.
  • restaurants and commercial areas.

Accessibility: via paved road.

Utilities: capacity for electrical city power, tap water and drainage, natural gas pipes.

Hazard free: Free of industrial or waste hazard withing 2km radius.

Flood risks: Very low risk, at least 100 meters away from any river nearby.
